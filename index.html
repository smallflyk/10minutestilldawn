10 Minutes Till Dawn

Survive the night in this intense bullet hell roguelike game

About The Game

10 Minutes Till Dawn is an intense survival horror bullet hell roguelike where your goal is simple: survive for 10 minutes against endless waves of eldritch horrors. Armed with various weapons and magical abilities, you must navigate the darkness and fend off the encroaching enemies before dawn arrives.

How To Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Your weapon fires automatically. Collect experience points from defeated enemies to level up and choose new upgrades. Survive until dawn to win!

Roguelike Elements

Each run is different with random upgrades and enemy patterns. Develop your own build strategy to overcome the night.

Bullet Hell Action

Dodge intricate patterns of enemy projectiles while returning fire in this fast-paced action game.

Upgrade System

Gain experience and unlock powerful upgrades and abilities to enhance your chances of survival.

Game Tips

Focus on movement and dodging first. Don't get cornered against the edges of the map. Prioritize upgrades that complement your playstyle. Some builds focus on raw damage, while others excel at area control.
